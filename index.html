About Me

A multidisciplinary professional dedicated to education and community service

I am Prem Raj Adhikari, a passionate educator and leader committed to transforming lives through quality education and community service. As the Principal and Founder of Oxford School, I have dedicated my career to creating an environment where students can thrive academically and personally.

My journey in education extends beyond the classroom as a Lecturer at College of Education Fulbari, where I shape the next generation of educators. I am honored to serve as Secretary at National PABSON Kaski, working towards the betterment of educational standards across the region.

Community service is close to my heart, and I actively contribute as an Executive Member at Fewatal Jaladhar Samraksyan Samitee, focusing on environmental conservation. Previously, I served as सहसचिव at वसन्त रूबिना फाउन्डेसन नेपाल, supporting various social causes.

Beyond my professional commitments, music flows through my veins. I believe that music and education share a common thread - both have the power to inspire, heal, and bring people together.
